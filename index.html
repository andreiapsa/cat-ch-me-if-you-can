Instructions

Neji, like every cat, hates vacuum cleaners and needs your help to run away from them.

  • Use the arrows (top, bottom, left and right) to move around
  • Neji, the cat, needs to stay away from the vacuum cleaners
  • If a vacuum cleaner hits Neji, the cat, he'll lose one life
  • Neji, the cat, should eat the cans of food to get more energy
  • If Neji, the cat, eats a can of food, he'll get one extra life
  • The game is over when Neji, the cat, has no more lives
