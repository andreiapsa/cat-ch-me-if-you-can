Instructions

Neji, like every cat, hates vacuum cleaners and needs your help to run away from them.

  • Use the arrows (top, bottom, left and right) to move around
  • Neji needs to stay away from the vacuum cleaners
  • If a vacuum cleaner hits Neji he'll lose 1 life
  • If Neji avoids a vacuum cleaner he'll get 1 point
  • Neji should eat the cans of food to get more energy
  • If Neji eats a can of food, he'll get 3 points
  • Every now and then you'll see a surprise super bonus
  • If Neji catches the super bonus, he'll get 2 extra lives and 10 points
  • The game is over when Neji has no more lives
